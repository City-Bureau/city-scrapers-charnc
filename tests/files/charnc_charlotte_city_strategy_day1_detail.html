Special Meeting Notice: City Council Annual Strategy Meeting (Day 1)

Pursuant to G.S. 143-318.12 (b)(2), (e) and Sec. 3.02 of the City of Charlotte Charter, the mayor has called a special meeting of the Charlotte City Council for Monday, March 2 and Tuesday, March 3, starting both days at 9 a.m. 

City Council will recess and reconvene at 6 pm on Monday, March 2, at the Charlotte Mecklenburg Government Center, 600 East Fourth Street, Charlotte, NC 28202. The purpose of these meetings is to hold the Annual Council Strategy Meeting and conduct further business, as necessary. 

Meeting agendas are available at City of Charlotte - Calendar (legistar.com).

The public can attend the meeting in-person or watch the meeting via live stream on YouTube on the GOV Channel(Spectrum Cable channel 1302, AT&T U-verse channel 99) or via the GOV Channel Streaming App, which can be downloaded from the Apple App Store or Google Play.
